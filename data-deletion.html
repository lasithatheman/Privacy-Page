Data Deletion Request

If you want to delete your data associated with our app, please follow these steps:

How to Request Data Deletion:

  1. Send an email to YOUR-EMAIL@example.com
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide your Facebook user ID or the email address you used to log in

What Data We Delete:

We will delete all personal information associated with your account, including:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your profile information
  • Any content or data you've created

Processing Time:

Data deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.
